# WealthRise Capitals - Website Information Requirements

Please provide the following information and assets to help us build your website effectively.

## 1. Brand Identity & Assets
- **Logos:** High-resolution logos in various formats (SVG, PNG with transparent background, standard, and mobile icons/favicons). *Note: Ensure you have both light and dark mode versions if applicable.*
- **Brand Guidelines:** Document detailing exact brand colors (Hex/RGB codes) and typography/fonts.
- **Tagline:** Confirmation of the official tagline (e.g., "GROWING WEALTH · CREATING FUTURES").

## 2. Company Information & "About Us"
- **Company History/Story:** A brief background of how and why the company was founded.
- **Mission & Vision Statements:** Core values and long-term goals of the company.
- **Team Members:**
  - High-quality professional headshots.
  - Names, job titles, and brief bios for key team members or founders.
  - Links to their professional LinkedIn profiles (optional).
- **Office Location(s):** Full physical address for Google Maps integration.
- **Contact Details:** Official phone numbers, general inquiry email address, and support email address.
- **Business Hours:** Operating hours and timezones.

## 3. Services & Offerings (Core Content)
- **List of Services:** A comprehensive list of all financial services offered (e.g., Wealth Management, Retirement Planning, Investment Advisory, Corporate Finance).
- **Service Descriptions:** Detailed, client-facing descriptions for each service (what it is, who it's for, and the benefits).
- **Process/How It Works:** Step-by-step explanation of what a client experiences when they start working with you.

## 4. Legal & Compliance
- **Terms and Conditions:** Legal terms for using the website.
- **Privacy Policy:** How client data is collected, used, and protected.
- **Disclaimer / Regulatory Disclosures:** Required financial disclaimers (e.g., "Past performance is not indicative of future results" or specific SEC/FINRA/local regulatory body licenses and registration numbers).

## 5. Social Proof & Media
- **Client Testimonials:** Quotes, names, and (if permitted) photos or video testimonials from satisfied clients.
- **Partnerships/Affiliations:** Logos of partnering institutions, custodian banks, or regulatory bodies you are members of.
- **Press/Media:** Links or PDFs to any news articles, awards, or press releases mentioning the company.

## 6. Functionality & Integration Details
- **Social Media Links:** URLs to official LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Facebook, etc.
- **Third-Party Tools/Portals:** Links to any external client login portals.
- **Lead Capture:** Specific information to collect on "Contact Us" or "Get Started" forms (e.g., Name, Email, Phone, Investable Assets).
- **Newsletter/Email Marketing:** Details of any email marketing platforms used (e.g., Mailchimp, HubSpot).

## 7. Visual Content
- **Photography:** Official office photos or specific stock image preferences.
- **Videos/Animations:** Any introductory videos or background videos to feature on the site.
